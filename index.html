QuickKart

QuickKart is an e-commerce web application created to provide a fast and smooth online shopping experience. Users can explore products, manage their cart, and place orders easily through a clean and efficient interface. The project emphasizes simplicity, speed, and organized product handling.

IoT-Powered Gas Leakage Alert and Industrial Safety System

This project is a real-time IoT-based safety solution designed to monitor hazardous gases and environmental conditions in industrial areas. The system provides instant alerts and triggers safety actions whenever gas levels exceed safe limits, helping to reduce risks and improve workplace safety.
